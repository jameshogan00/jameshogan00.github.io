About this site

The intention of this site was to practice my front end, UX and UI design skills. With the proliferation of the internet in recent years, people can suffer from "information overload" due to the amount of web stages they constantly browse through on a daily basis.

I therefore took a minimalist approach and used as much white space (black for the image gallery) as possible around text-heavy areas in order to keep the user focused on the most important aspect of every website: content.

My aim is to expand this site and continue its "story telling" nature in order to keep the user engaged throughout.

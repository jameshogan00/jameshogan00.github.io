Awards

The people of Knockbridge take pride in maintaining a clean and environmentally friendly environment. Please take a look below to see how our efforts have been recognised at both a regional and national level.

Awards

  • 2013 - Louth Litter League Gold Medal winner
  • 2008 - Ireland's Best Kept Village
  • 2007 - National Tidy Towns Competition - Gold Medal Award
  • 2006 - National Tidy Towns Competition - Silver Medal Award
  • 2004 - Louth County Council Tidy District Award
  • 2001 - Louth County Council Tidy District Award

Environmental

  • Lisroland View housing estate - Since its foundation in 2000, the Residents Association have maintained the green areas and planted trees.
  • Completed the STEM project in accordance with EU guidelines in 2007.
  • Developed a green space at Mullaghbuoy (Stonetrough)
  • Planted trees both sides of the Dundalk Road from the village towards Ballinclare

Awards like these don't come easy. Why not take a look at our projects page to see what we have done to enhance our town along with what's in store for 2014 and beyond.

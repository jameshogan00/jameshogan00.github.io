WE'RE LOSING GROUND

Lets do something about it

Here's the problem

Mother nature is angry. We witness how she vents her frustration every time we turn on the news: Tsumanis, floods, typhoons, forest fires in northern parts of Russia - it's clear that no one is safe from harm, and we only have ourselves to blame. The latest UN report on the implications of unmitigated climate change vindicates how real the threat of global warming really is.

"It is extremely likely that human influence has been the dominant cause of observed warming since 1950, with the level of confidence having increased since the fourth report."

The issue is often percieved to be a far off matter to be dealt with in decades to come. The recent financial crisis has taken priority over tackling what cosmologist Stephen Hawking describes as "one of the greatest threats posed to the future of human-kind and the world."

We're running out of time in our global effort to combat climate change. The scientific evidence behind global warming is becoming more transparent. Historical data gives us a clear indication that human activity is largely responsibe for the surge in carbon emissions over the past 100 years. There's still a chance for us to turn things around before it gets too late. Change will be granular without you taking the initiative. Educating your children on the matter from an early age is probably the most effective thing you will ever teach them to do. Take a bicycle to work instead of a car - the health and environmental benefits of doing so will be enormous.

THIS IS YOUR PLANET
Don't destroy it when you have a chance to save it

You can stop this

The earth's destiny is in the palm of your hand. Little things make such a big difference and every one of us can play a role in preventing global warming. Do you live close to your workplace? Why not cycle instead of taking the car. Are your parents retired and looking for something to do? Suggest they try growing their own fruit and vegetables. Do you have children with a passion for reading? Why not buy them a kids book on environmental awareness like this one. There is so much you can do to help and you'll be surprised at the enjoyment you get out of it. Please make a difference -TODAY.
